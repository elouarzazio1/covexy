About
Covexy — The AI that acts before you ask
Covexy
v1.0.0
Covexy is a proactive AI that runs silently on your Mac and tells you what matters before you think to ask. It watches your screen, understands your context, and speaks up only when it has something that genuinely changes what you do next.
Roadmap

Privacy
Screen observations are processed via OpenRouter to generate insights. No data is stored on external servers, sold, or used for training. Everything else stays on your device.
